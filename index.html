Welcome to Wild Rescues

Wild Rescues is a registered non-profit organization located in the heart of the city. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and release wild animals back into their natural habitats. We provide a safe haven for injured, orphaned, or displaced wildlife, offering them the care and medical attention they need to thrive once again.

Our dedicated team of veterinarians and volunteers work tirelessly to ensure that every animal that comes through our doors receives the best possible care. From tiny baby birds to large mammals, we are committed to the well-being of all creatures, great and small.

Join us in our efforts to protect and preserve our local wildlife. Whether through donations, volunteering, or simply spreading the word, your support makes a difference in the lives of the animals we serve.
