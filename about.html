What we do

We provide specialized care for injured and orphaned wildlife with the goal of releasing them back into the wild. Our facility is equipped to handle a variety of species, from birds of prey to small mammals.

Our Team

Our team consists of licensed wildlife rehabilitators, veterinarians, and dedicated volunteers who share a passion for animal welfare and conservation.

How you can help

As a non-profit organization, we rely on the generosity of our community. You can support us through donations, volunteering your time, or participating in our partnership programs.
